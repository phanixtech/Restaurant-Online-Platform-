BMI Calculator

Get a quick snapshot of your health status. Use it as a starting point, not a finish line.

--
--
UnderweightNormalOverweightObese
🌿 Underweight (BMI < 18.5)
You may need to gain weight through muscle mass. Our nutrition team can design a muscle-building meal plan for you.
✅ Normal Weight (18.5 – 24.9)
You're in a healthy range. Focus on maintaining it with consistent exercise and a balanced diet. Now's a great time to work on strength goals.
⚡ Overweight (25 – 29.9)
You're slightly above optimal range. A blend of cardio and resistance training can help you drop the excess and build lean muscle.
🔥 Obese (BMI ≥ 30)
We understand this journey is hard. Our coaches specialize in safe, evidence-based fat loss with full emotional support and a no-judgment culture.
